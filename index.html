Most Common Solutions

Software

  • Updating: Update drivers, software, and operating systems to resolve various issues.
  • Reinstalling: Sometimes a fresh installation of software or the OS can solve problems.
  • Antivirus: The issue you are facing can also be a Malware or Virus infection.
  • Clearing: If your computer is slow. Clearing temporary files and cache helps
  • SFC: Running SFC (System File Checker) helps you with operating system issues

Hardware

  • Restarting: This is the most common solution for many computer issues.
  • Cleaning: Clean the dust off your fans and heat sinks, ensure proper ventilation.
  • Connections: Ensure that your cables are correctly plugged in and are in good condition.
  • Backups: Keep your important files in a backup. This allows your files to be safer.
  • Errors: During blue screens you get an error code. By searching up this code your issue is releaved.
