About the Project

This website exists to help you get through simple issues surrounding your computer.

Most Common Solutions

  • Restarting: This is the most common solution for many computer issues.

  • Updating: Update drivers, software, and operating systems to resolve various issues.

  • Reinstalling: Sometimes a fresh installation of software or the OS can solve problems.

  • Antivirus: The issue you are facing can also be a Malware or Virus infection.

  • Clearing: If your computer is slow. Clearing temporary files and cache helps.
